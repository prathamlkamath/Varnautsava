About The Event

About The Event

Varnotsava is a vibrant cultural and literary festival organized by SMVITM, showcasing the creativity and talent of students through various events like debates, quizzes, creative writing, and artistic performances. It serves as a platform to celebrate linguistic diversity and foster a spirit of expression, innovation, and camaraderie among participants.

Where

SMVITM, Bantakal

When

Thursday to Sunday
20-23 March
